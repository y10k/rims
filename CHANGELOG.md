0.2.9

Released on 2019-12-12.

### Added
- Add `umask(2)` configuration parameter. [#29](https://github.com/y10k/rims/issues/29)

  [#30](https://github.com/y10k/rims/issues/30)
  [#33](https://github.com/y10k/rims/issues/33)
